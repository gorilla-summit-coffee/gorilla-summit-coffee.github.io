Our Story

Gorilla Summit Coffee was founded to provide small-scale coffee farmers in Uganda access to global coffee markets, top-grade coffee plants, and modern coffee farming practices.

We are proud to support over 100 Ugandan farmers, helping them sell their coffee to the US and Europe.
